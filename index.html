About Me

I am Dustin Halsey, an aspiring Game Designer. I first began developing video games Sophomore year of high school and have been creating games and improving my skills ever since. Currently, I am pursuing a Computer Science: Computer Game Design Bachelors's degree at the University of California: Santa Cruz with the intent to transition my skills into commercial game development. While my specialties lie in the programming side, I make it my mission to be well versed in all aspects of the game design process. This includes development across different environments such as Unity, Gamemaker Studio, and the Javascript framework Phaser as well as thorough experience modeling and animating using Blender.
