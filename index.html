Welcome!

I am a computer game deisgner who recently graduated from University of California: Santa Cruz with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science: Computer Game Design. Fueled by my lifelong passion towards games, I first began developing video games Sophomore year of high school and have been creating games and improving my skills ever since.
     As a lifelong gamer and a tinkerer at heart, I crave the satisfaction that comes with really getting down to the nitty gritty of how things work and use that knowledge to construct something that I can truely call my own. While primarily focusing on the programming side of things, I make it my mission to be well versed in all aspects of the game design process including 3D modeling, animation, sound design, and more.
     Here, you can find my résumé and a portfolio of projects on which i've worked as well as a conglomeration of side projects i've created for fun. If you'd like to get in touch, I'd love to chat!
